Why Research Matters

The more you understand the market, the better your proposal becomes.

Professional Research Workflow

Professional innovators don't randomly create ideas. They follow a structured research process to understand problems, discover opportunities, and validate solutions before writing a proposal.

👀

Step 1

Observe daily problems faced by individuals, businesses, students, developers, or organizations.

🔍

Step 2

Research existing solutions using search engines, websites, applications and technology platforms.

📊

Step 3

Compare competitors, identify missing features and discover opportunities for improvement.

💡

Step 4

Create your own innovative solution based on your research instead of copying others.

Where Should You Research?

Always verify your ideas using multiple trusted sources before preparing your proposal.

🌐

Google Search

Search for existing businesses, websites, services and similar ideas.

💻

GitHub

Check whether developers have already created open-source projects similar to your idea.

🚀

Product Hunt

Explore newly launched startups, SaaS products and innovative technology platforms.

📱

App Stores

Search Google Play and Apple App Store for similar applications.

✅ Beginner Research Checklist

✔ Understand the problem before thinking about a solution.

✔ Search Google for similar products.

✔ Search GitHub for related projects.

✔ Explore Product Hunt for startup ideas.

✔ Check Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

✔ Read reviews to discover what users dislike.

✔ Write notes about missing features and opportunities.

✔ Think about how your solution can be better, easier or faster.

⚠ Common Beginner Mistakes

❌ Copying existing ideas without adding value.

❌ Skipping market research.

❌ Creating unrealistic business concepts.

❌ Ignoring user needs and focusing only on features.

❌ Submitting proposals without verifying originality.

Successful innovators always research first, validate their ideas, and continuously improve based on user needs.

Research Tools & Market Validation

Professional innovators never depend on a single source. They collect information from multiple trusted platforms to understand competitors, market demand, user feedback and future opportunities.

🌐

Google Search

Search existing websites, businesses, articles, communities and services related to your idea.

💻

GitHub

Discover open-source software and developer projects that already solve similar problems.

🚀

Product Hunt

Explore recently launched startups, SaaS products and AI platforms from around the world.

📱

App Stores

Review similar mobile applications, user ratings and feature requests before creating your proposal.

📊 Validate Your Market

Creating an idea is only the beginning. Successful innovators first confirm that users actually need the solution.

Ask yourself whether people already search for this problem, whether existing products satisfy users, and whether businesses would pay for your solution.

If there is demand and current solutions are weak, you have identified a valuable opportunity.

What Should You Analyze?

Before preparing your proposal, carefully evaluate every competitor.

⭐

Features

List the important features every competitor provides.

👍

Strengths

Understand why users like existing products.

👎

Weaknesses

Read reviews to identify missing features and common complaints.

💡

Opportunity

Think about how your innovation can solve these problems more effectively.

📝 Practice Exercise

Choose any existing website, mobile application or SaaS platform.

Research at least three competitors and write down:

✔ What they do well.

✔ What users complain about.

✔ Which features are missing.

✔ How your idea could improve the experience.

This simple exercise helps you think like a professional product designer instead of simply copying existing ideas.

Writing A Professional Proposal

After completing your research, organize your findings into a clear and professional proposal. A well-structured proposal helps reviewers quickly understand your idea, its value, and its potential.

📝

Project Title

Choose a short, meaningful and memorable name that clearly represents your innovation.

❗

Problem Statement

Explain the real-world problem your idea solves and why existing solutions are insufficient.

💡

Your Solution

Describe how your product, website, application or service solves the identified problem.

👥

Target Users

Identify who will benefit from your solution and explain why they would choose it.

📄 Recommended Proposal Structure

A professional proposal should include the following sections:

✔ Project Title

✔ Executive Summary

✔ Problem Statement

✔ Existing Solutions

✔ Market Research

✔ Your Innovation

✔ Target Audience

✔ Unique Features

✔ Revenue Model

✔ Future Improvements

✔ Conclusion

Professional Writing Tips

Clear communication is just as important as a great idea.

✍️

Be Clear

Use simple language and explain your idea in a way that anyone can understand.

📊

Use Evidence

Support your proposal with research, statistics, user feedback and competitor analysis whenever possible.

🎯

Stay Focused

Avoid unnecessary information. Keep every section relevant to your innovation.

📖

Organize Well

Use headings, bullet points and short paragraphs to make your proposal easy to read.

⚠ Common Proposal Mistakes

❌ Writing without research.

❌ Copying ideas from existing platforms.

❌ Not explaining the problem clearly.

❌ Ignoring competitors.

❌ Making unrealistic claims without evidence.

❌ Poor formatting and grammar.

The strongest proposals are clear, realistic, well-researched and focused on solving genuine user problems.

Preparing Your Submission

Before submitting your work to CoinGrid, take a few minutes to review everything carefully. A well-prepared submission creates a positive first impression and improves the overall quality of your proposal.

📄

Use Google Docs

Prepare your proposal using Google Docs with proper headings, formatting, bullet points and clear explanations.

🔗

Share Your Document

Set your Google Docs permission to Anyone with the link so the CoinGrid review team can access your proposal.

📝

Review Everything

Read your proposal one final time to correct grammar, spelling mistakes and incomplete information before submission.

🚀

Submit Confidently

Only submit when you are satisfied that your proposal is original, complete and professionally presented.

✅ Final Submission Checklist

Before clicking the Submit button, make sure you have completed every step below.

✔ Completed proper market research.

✔ Compared existing competitors.

✔ Clearly explained the problem.

✔ Described your unique solution.

✔ Identified your target audience.

✔ Explained your business or revenue model.

✔ Checked grammar and spelling.

✔ Shared your Google Docs with Anyone with the link permission.

✔ Verified that your idea is original or significantly improves existing solutions.

📢 Before You Submit

Remember that CoinGrid values quality over quantity. A single well-researched and professionally written proposal has a much better chance of approval than multiple incomplete or copied ideas.

Take your time, review your work carefully and submit only when you are confident in your research and presentation.

Innovation begins with curiosity, grows through research and succeeds through execution.

📄

Insufficient Research
