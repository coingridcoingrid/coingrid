Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about CoinGrid, earnings, withdrawals, sponsors, and platform security.

User FAQs

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button and complete the registration form using your email address.

How do I earn rewards?

Complete available tasks, surveys, app installs, and promotional activities to earn points and rewards.

Can I use CoinGrid on mobile?

Yes. CoinGrid is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Payment FAQs

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum withdrawal amount depends on the selected payment method and platform settings.

How long do withdrawals take?

Most withdrawals are processed within 24–72 hours after approval.

Which payment methods are supported?

CoinGrid supports UPI, Bank Transfer, PayPal, and Cryptocurrency withdrawals.

Sponsor FAQs

How do sponsors create campaigns?
Sponsors can create campaigns from their sponsor dashboard by setting rewards, budgets, and target audiences.
How is campaign budget calculated?
Budget depends on reward amount, number of completions, and campaign duration.
Can I pause a campaign?
Yes. Sponsors can pause and resume campaigns anytime from the task management page.

Security FAQs

Is CoinGrid secure?
CoinGrid uses secure authentication, encrypted communication, and fraud prevention systems.
How is user data protected?
User information is stored securely and only used according to our Privacy Policy.
Can accounts be suspended?
Accounts violating platform rules may be suspended or permanently removed.
