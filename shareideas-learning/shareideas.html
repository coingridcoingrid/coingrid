⚠ Important Before You Continue

This is not a website development task.

You are not expected to build an application, webpage, or software prototype.

Instead, your goal is to create a professional document describing one or more unique online earning ideas that are practical, useful, and not already available.

Before submitting any idea, research whether similar services already exist using Google, AI assistants, technology blogs, startup directories, and online communities.

Ideas that simply copy existing platforms or promise unrealistic income are unlikely to be accepted.

Focus on innovation, usefulness, feasibility, and long-term value for users.
