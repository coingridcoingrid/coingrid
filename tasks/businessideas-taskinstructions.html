Innovation Research Framework

Follow this simple framework before preparing your proposal. Strong ideas are built through research, creativity, and solving genuine problems.

1️⃣ Identify A Real Problem

Observe industries, businesses, or daily activities and identify challenges people experience. Great innovations always begin with a real problem.

2️⃣ Research Existing Solutions

Search Google, GitHub, Product Hunt, App Store, Play Store, startup websites, and AI search tools to understand what already exists.

3️⃣ Discover The Gap

Find weaknesses in current products and think about how your solution can be faster, smarter, easier, or more useful.

4️⃣ Design Your Solution

Explain how your website, application, SaaS platform, AI tool, cloud service, or business will solve the problem better than existing solutions.

5️⃣ Verify Your Innovation

Before submitting, make sure your idea is original or clearly explains how it significantly improves existing products.

6️⃣ Prepare Your Proposal

Write a professional proposal including the problem, solution, target users, business model, expected benefits, and future growth opportunities.

How Your Proposal Will Be Reviewed

Every submission is carefully evaluated to identify innovative, practical and scalable ideas with real-world business potential.

📥 Step 1 — Proposal Submission

Your Google Docs proposal is securely received and assigned to the CoinGrid Innovation Review Team for evaluation.

🔍 Step 2 — Originality Verification

We search existing websites, applications, startups, GitHub repositories and technology platforms to verify your idea is original or provides significant improvements.

📊 Step 3 — Market Research Review

Our team evaluates market demand, competitor analysis, business opportunity and whether users would genuinely benefit from your solution.

⚙️ Step 4 — Technical Feasibility

We determine whether your concept can realistically be developed using current technologies and available resources.

🚀 Step 5 — Development Approval

Approved ideas are shortlisted for future development based on originality, feasibility, business value and long-term growth potential.

🌍 Step 6 — Product Launch

Selected ideas may be developed, tested and launched as real CoinGrid products for users around the world.
