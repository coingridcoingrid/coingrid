Part 2 — Finding Winning Article Ideas

Every successful article starts with a valuable idea. Before writing anything, learn how to find ideas people actually want to read.

💡 Solve a real problem. People search for solutions every day. Choose problems you can genuinely help solve.
🔍 Research first. Search Google, YouTube, Reddit and Quora. If hundreds of people ask the same question, it may be a good topic.
📈 Look for growing trends. AI, freelancing, remote work, small business ideas and productivity continue attracting readers.
❌ Avoid copied ideas. Don't rewrite another website. Create your own examples, screenshots, methods, and explanations.
🎯 Target beginners. Articles that are easy to understand usually receive better engagement.
❤️ Think about value. Ask yourself: "Will someone finish this article and learn something useful?" If yes, you're on the right track.

The CoinGrid Research Framework

Step 1: Choose a topic that solves a real problem.

Step 2: Research at least five reliable sources.

Step 3: Take notes in your own words.

Step 4: Add your own experience, examples, screenshots, or workflow.

Step 5: Never copy paragraphs from another website.

Idea Validation Checklist

✅ People search for this topic.
✅ It provides practical value.
✅ You can explain it clearly.
✅ You have researched enough.
✅ It isn't copied.
✅ You would proudly publish it.
